Mary kom, an Indian boxer hailing from Manipur, is a five time world Amateur Boxing champion. She is the only India boxerto have qualified fro the 2012 summer olympics, competing in the flyweight 51Kg category and winning the bronze medal. She has also been ranked as No.4 AIBA World Women's Ranking Flyweight category. She became the first Indian woman boxer to get a Gold Medal in the Asian Games in 2014 in Icheon South Korea.
